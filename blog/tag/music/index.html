12th Annual North City Jazz Walk

Posted August 6, 2019 to Events

When
Tuesday, August 13, 2019, 6:00pm – 10:00pm
Where
North City along 15th Ave NE

Dance under the setting sun and stars to the strains of old and new jazz, enjoy delicious “Jazz Bites” food, tours of Shoreline Fire District’s fire truck, a beer and wine garden, and more — all at North City’s thirteenth annual Jazz Walk

The dining starts on 6pm, the music starts at 6:30pm.

Tickets are $20 in advance and $25 on the day of show.

More information can be found at northcityjazzwalk.org.

North City Blues Fest

Posted September 30, 2015 to Events

When
Thursday, October 29, 2015, 6:00pm – 10:30pm
Where
North City along 15th Ave NE

This new event came into being based on the feedback from 2015 Jazz Walk. Overwhelmingly, Jazz Walk attendees wanted an opportunity to have other musical gatherings more often than the annual event.

Five venues in North City will be participating this year. They will begin serving food at 6:00. The music will begin at 7:00 and there will be three forty-five minute sets. Tickets are $12.

The Blues Fest is sponsored by the North City Business Association. See their website for further details.

9th Annual North City Jazz Walk

Posted February 4, 2015 to Events Updated July 13, 2015

When
Tuesday, August 11, 2015, 6:30pm – 10:00pm
Where
North City along 15th Ave NE

Take a stroll along 15th Avenue NE in Shoreline and enjoy live jazz at one great venue after another. This year, come early and enjoy “Jazz Bites” — delicious food items specifically provided for the event.

Performers include:

  • Greta Matassa with Critical Mass
  • The Susan Pascal Quartet
  • Pearl Django
  • Sonando
  • Janette West
  • The Bill Anschell Trio
  • The Jacqueline Tabor Quartet
  • The Freddie James Duo
  • Hook Me Up

Tickets are available on-line. Buy now, tickets are limited.

The jazz bites start at 6:00pm; the music starts at 6:30pm in the outdoor venues and at 7:00pm in the indoor venues.

More information can be found at northcityjazzwalk.org.

If you’d like to volunteer at the event, contact Charlotte Haines.
